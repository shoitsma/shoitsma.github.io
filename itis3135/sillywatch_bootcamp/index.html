Sillywatch Bootcamp Home Page

Learning Outcomes:

  • Awareness of the main game modes and the objectives of each
  • Understanding the stages of a typical fight
  • Comprehension of the distinctions between roles and how to play each role effectively
  • Familarity with each hero and their general playstyle
  • Approaches to various hero matchups
  • Recognition of different types of team compositions
  • Identifying when to use an ultimate ability
  • Knowing how to improve at the game
